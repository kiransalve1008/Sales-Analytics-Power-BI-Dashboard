# Sales-Analytics-Power-BI-Dashboard
This Sales Analytics dashboard is developed using Power BI to analyze customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and sales performance. The dashboard helps businesses understand who their customers are, where sales are coming from, and which segments drive profitability, enabling better sales and marketing strategies.

**Sales Analytics Power BI Dashboard**
**Project Overview**
This Sales Analytics dashboard is developed using Power BI to analyze customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and sales performance.
The dashboard helps businesses understand who their customers are, where sales are coming from, and which segments drive profitability, enabling better sales and marketing strategies.

Step-by-Step Process Used
1.	Data Collection
•	Imported sales dataset into Power BI Desktop
•	Data includes customer demographics, orders, income, occupation, and country
2.	Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
•	Removed duplicates and unnecessary columns
•	Standardized categorical values (education, marital status, occupation)
•	Corrected data types (numeric & text)
•	Created calculated columns for grouping
3.	Data Modeling
•	Designed optimized data model for analytics
•	Ensured clean relationships between tables (where applicable)
4.	DAX Measures Created
•	Total Profit
•	Average Income
•	Average Age
•	Total Orders
•	Purchase Share (%)
5.	Dashboard Design
•	Used KPI cards, bar charts, donut charts, and column charts
•	Applied clean color theme for readability
•	Designed layout focused on customer insights
________________________________________
Key Insights & Analysis
•	Customers with Bachelor’s and Graduate degrees contribute the highest purchase share
•	Male and Female customers show nearly equal purchase behavior
•	Married customers contribute slightly higher purchase share than single customers
•	Management and Professional occupations have higher average income
•	United States leads in total orders, followed by Australia and Germany
